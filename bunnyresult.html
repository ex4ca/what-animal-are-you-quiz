You are a bunny!


art by @cremechii on twitter

You are a loving and affectionate person. Although you can
be shy at first, you can make lots of close friends.
Despite having many friends, you make sure they all feel as loved
as one another, making sure you never pick favourites.
Your loved ones always feel comfortable and safe around you.
Bunnies are bashful, loving creatures who will never leave your side.
You should be very proud to be like one!
